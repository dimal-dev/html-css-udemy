<Question 8>
  <Description>
    What is margin collapsing?
  </Description>
  <Answer 1>
    <Is Correct>Yes</Is Correct>
    <Content>When vertical margins of two elements combine into one</Content>
    <Explanation>Why? When two elements’ margins meet with no content between them, only the largest margin is used.</Explanation>
  </Answer 1>
  <Answer 2>
    <Is Correct>No</Is Correct>
    <Content>When margin shrinks if the browser is too small</Content>
    <Explanation>Why? Margin doesn’t shrink due to browser size—only layouts adjust.</Explanation>
  </Answer 2>
  <Answer 3>
    <Is Correct>No</Is Correct>
    <Content>When margins are removed entirely by the browser</Content>
    <Explanation>Why? Margins are not removed, they overlap and the largest one is applied.</Explanation>
