<Question 3>
    <Description>
        What’s the purpose of the Inspect button (the small cursor icon in DevTools)?
    </Description>
    <Answer 1>

        <Explanation>Why? The Inspect tool links live page elements to their HTML/CSS in DevTools.</Explanation>
    </Answer 1>
    <Answer 2>
        <Is Correct>No</Is Correct>
        <Content>It copies an element’s color value to the clipboard</Content>
        <Explanation>Why? You must use Computed tab or styles to find and copy color values.</Explanation>
    </Answer 2>
    <Answer 3>
        <Is Correct>No</Is Correct>
        <Content>It enables JavaScript editing</Content>
        <Explanation>Why? Inspect mode focuses on HTML and CSS, not scripting.</Explanation>
    </Answer 3>

        <Explanation>Why? Computed tab shows resolved styles and lets you filter to find exact values.</Explanation>
    </Answer 2>
    <Answer 3>
        <Is Correct>No</Is Correct>
        <Content>Console > Log background color</Content>
        <Explanation>Why? Console allows custom scripts, but isn’t meant for direct style inspection.</Explanation>
    </Answer 3>
